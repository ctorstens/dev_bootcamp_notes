# Lifestyle
---

## Sleep

- Amount: 6.5 - 7.5 hours a night
- Nap. 20 - 30 minutes a day
- Develop a sleep ritual
  - read **Fiction**
  - go for a walk
  - tie waking up to an immediate event afterwards
- Exhaust yourself mentally (not hard), and physically (harder)
