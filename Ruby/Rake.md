# Rake
---

## Rake Commands

- ```$ rake console```
- ```$ rake db:drop```
- ```$ rake db:create```
- ```$ rake db:migrate```
- ```$ rake db:seed```
- ```$ rake db:drop && rake db:create && rake db:migrate && rake db:seed```

## Rake Generations

- ```$ rake generate:migration NAME=create_users```
- ```$ rake generate:model NAME=user```
